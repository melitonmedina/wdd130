Overview

Purpose

Provide with a thrilling and memorable experience to both those new to rafting and the seasoned ones.

Audience

The service is for anyone who is at least twelve year old.

  • New to the rafting world? There is nothing to worry about; a team of highly experienced rafters will be available to provide all necessary instruction, answer any questions, and address concerns. They will also recommend the best rafting trip options.
  • How long do trips usually last? Trips range from 2.5 to 5 hours.
  • What medical conditions will exclude me? It is highly recommended not to participate if there are heart conditions to consider. However, you are always encouraged to speak with your doctor.
