Overview

Purpose

Provide with a thrilling and memorable experience to both those new to rafting and the seasoned ones.

Audience

The service is for anyone who is at least twelve year old.

  • New to the rafting world? There is nothing to worry about a team of highly experienced drafters will be available to provide all necessary instrunction and to answer any questions and address concerns. They will also recommend the best rafting trip option.
  • How long do trips usually go for? Trips range from 2.5hrs to 5hrs.
  • What medical conditions will exclude me? It highly recommended to no participate when there is a heart conditions to consider. But always you are encouraged to speak with your doctor.
